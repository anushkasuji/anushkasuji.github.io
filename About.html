OUR MISSION

Acting as a student lead programming board, the MAC Board strives to provide inclusive and accessible opportunities through cultural, recreational, and educational programs for the diverse MassArt student body. They foster a positive environment for the MassArt community by encouraging creative expression and engagement of all students to enhance their overall experience.


WHAT IS A MAC MINI?

MAC Mini’s helps in manage events and are suppose to create posters. They are supposed to help organise stuff and help in needing the supplies. They are supposed to come in some of the meetings and see what’s going on and if they can come onboard with something. They are the important resources for MAC. They are also paid minimum wage.
